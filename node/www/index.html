SL1 is not a system of execution.

It is a system that prevents execution from being inferred.
Meaning

Representation is not reality.

Facts, proofs, graphs, obligations, and intents describe meaning. They do not mutate state by themselves.

Authority

Authority is explicit lineage.

Capability, ControlGrant, IntentApproval, and Authorization explain why an action may be honored.

Settlement

Only settlement mutates state.

Transactions and settlement operations record what actually changed and why it can be verified later.
